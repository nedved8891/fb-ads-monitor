📊 FB Ads Monitor

FB Ads Monitor is a Google Sheets Add-on that automatically monitors your Facebook advertising campaigns and pauses underperforming ads based on your custom performance thresholds.

What does this app do?

FB Ads Monitor connects to the Facebook Marketing API to retrieve real-time performance data for your ads and ad sets. It evaluates each ad against your configured thresholds for cost per lead, cost per registration, and cost per deposit — and automatically pauses ads that are not performing well.

Key Features

📈 Automated Monitoring — Checks your campaigns every 30-60 minutes automatically
🚨 Smart Kill Logic — Pauses ads based on spend vs. leads/registrations/deposits thresholds
📱 Telegram Notifications — Sends instant alerts when ads are paused
📊 Dashboard — Visual overview of monitoring status and kill history
🔑 Multi-Account Support — Monitor multiple Facebook ad accounts with different tokens

How it works

  1. Install the add-on in Google Sheets
  2. Configure your Facebook access token and Telegram bot in the Config sheet
  3. Add campaigns or ad sets to monitor in the ManagedAdsets sheet
  4. Set your performance thresholds (max CPL, max CPR, max CPD)
  5. Enable automatic triggers and let the system monitor your ads 24/7
